Beside my main alumni community,
in my mind, there are two parts of alumni

* Where alumni come from: faculty, and department.

* Where currently alumni belong: region (e.g city), job, hobby

I can Imagine in the near future, that my alumni community will grow.
As each part can create their own independent community.
The issue is how to maintain main identity,
without sacrificing each part identities.
So I decide to create a 'what-if' collection of logo.
Inspired by this guidance:

* <http://fisip.ui.ac.id/informasi-publik/panduan-penggunaan-logo-universitas-indonesia.html>

Of course you can replace the logo with your own,
and replace the text with your own community,
and replace color to match your community's identity.

These UNOFFICIAL logos is pure just an initiative.
It is NEVER BEEN APPROVED by alumni organization.
So use it with your own risk.

Enjoy

-- -- --

## Design

It is so simple that, no need to put each stuff in its layer
However, you can still change the color easily.
Or add patter by yourself.

### Typography

As usual, I always use free font. 

* Trajan Pro, Trajan is Standard in my Campus.

### Document Properties

**Size**: 1200px width, 1200px height
